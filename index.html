FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

This list will continue to be updated

How can I get to the church if I am guest from out of town?

A complimentary shuttle bus will be available from the Union League Club to St. John Cantius and back to the Union League Club after the ceremony.
Please email help@owenandmegan.us if you are interested so your name can be added to the passenger list.
We will be posting departure times from the Union League Club to St. John Cantius soon.

What time should I arrive at the ceremony?

The ceremony will start at 2pm, however please plan to arrive half an hour early to leave time for seating and to enjoy the prelude music.

Where should guests who are driving to the church and reception park?

There is street parking and a small lot at St. John Cantius. Also, there is a parking garage next to the Union League Club at 318 S. Federal Street.

Will there be consideration for special diets at the reception?

Please email help@owenandmegan.us your dietary restrictions as soon as possble.

Where are you registered for gifts?

Come back at the end of March! It will be here.

If you have further questions please email help@owenandmegan.us
